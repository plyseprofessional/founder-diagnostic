Plyse Professional Services — Founder Scale System
Phase 0
Founder Diagnostic
A structured assessment to map where the founder is holding the business back — and where the highest-leverage interventions lie.

This diagnostic is confidential and intended for use within your Plyse Professional Services engagement. There are no right or wrong answers — respond based on how things actually are, not how you wish they were. Honest answers produce the most useful results. The tool will generate a scored profile and a prioritised action map.

0 of 24 answered
A
Decision Load
How many decisions flow through you — and what kind. This reveals the primary drag on your time and energy.
A1 On a typical working day, how many decisions do you make that you feel only you can make?
HealthyCritical
A2 Which types of decisions do you regularly make yourself? (Select all that apply)
A3 How often do team members interrupt your work to ask for decisions or approvals?
A4 Briefly describe the last decision you made that you believe someone else could have — but didn't.
B
Control and Delegation
Whether real authority has been handed off — or merely the appearance of it.
B1 When you assign a task to a team member, how confident are you that it will be completed correctly without your follow-up?
B2 Does anyone in your business have the authority to spend money or make a financial commitment without your prior approval?
B3 If you were unavailable for two weeks, how would the business cope?
C
Systems and Processes
Whether the business runs on documented, repeatable methods — or on the founder's memory and intuition.
C1 How much of the way you do things in this business is written down anywhere?
C2 When a new staff member joins, how do they learn what to do?
C3 How consistent is the quality of your product or service delivery across different staff or locations?
D
Organisation and Functions
Whether roles, responsibilities and functional boundaries exist — even informally.
D1 Does every person in your business have a clearly defined role with specific responsibilities?
D2 Which of these functions exist in any meaningful form in your business? (Select all that apply)
D3 Does anyone other than you hold a management or supervisory role with real authority over others?
E
Performance Visibility
Whether the business has any real-time view of how it is performing — beyond the founder's gut feel.
E1 How do you know if your business had a good week?
E2 Which of these performance measures do you track regularly? (Select all that apply)
E3 How often do you produce or review a financial report (profit & loss, cash flow)?
F
Founder Readiness
The honest truth about appetite for change — the most important variable of all.
F1 How would you honestly describe your relationship with control in this business?
F2 What is your primary motivation for seeking support right now?
F3 On a scale of 1–5, how ready are you to invest time and energy in restructuring how this business operates over the next 3–6 months?
F4 What do you most fear about delegating or sharing control?
Responses are scored locally and saved securely to Plyse records.
